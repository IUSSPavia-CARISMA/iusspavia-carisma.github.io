CARISMA is organising a [cycle of seminars on understanding and managing climate risk](https://www.iusspavia.it/it/eventi/cycle-seminars-understanding-and-managing-climate-risk-march-12-may-21-2026), running from March 12 to May 21, 2026, at IUSS Pavia (Palazzo Broletto) and online.

The series offers an overview of the scientific tools available to citizens, stakeholders, and decision-makers for adapting to climate change.
