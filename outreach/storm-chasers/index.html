Storm Chasers

Build the storm. Destroy the landmark. Outlast your rivals.

How to play

A severe storm needs four ingredients at once: Wind Shear, Moisture, Instability, and Lift. All teams attack one shared landmark (20 health) — its health only ever goes down.

  1. Each team starts with 4 random cards in hand — either storm ingredients or protection.
  2. On your turn your team answers a quiz question. Correct → you draw a card. Incorrect → no card.
  3. Then act: launch a storm (if you hold the ingredients) or pass.
  4. Mild storm (1 of each ingredient) deals 5 damage. Severe storm (2 of each) deals 10 damage. Launching uses up those ingredient cards.
  5. Interception: when you attack, any other team holding a Protection card may intercept — it cuts the storm by 3 damage and lets that team steal one random ingredient you just used. Only one team can intercept each storm.
  6. A storm always deals at least 1 damage, even if intercepted.
  7. Winning: the team whose storm drops the landmark's health to 0 wins. If it survives all 6 rounds, the team that dealt the most total damage wins.
