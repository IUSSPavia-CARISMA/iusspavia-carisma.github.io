My PhD focuses on the evaluation and improvement of crop models as impact models, with particular emphasis on their resilience in predicting the effects of environmental and climate change. The research also explores adaptation strategies and resource optimization.

My work integrates field observations and process-based crop models to better understand climate-related risks and to support evidence-based agricultural adaptation strategies.
