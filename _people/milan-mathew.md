Milan is a PhD candidate in the Sustainable Development and Climate Change (PhD SDC) program at IUSS Pavia, under the supervision of Prof. Giorgia Fosser. Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering and a Master’s in Climate Science, his research bridges the gap between climate science and energy systems engineering. Milan’s doctoral work focuses on assessing the impacts of climate change on the renewable energy sector. By leveraging high-resolution, convection-permitting climate model ensembles, his findings provide actionable insights to guide technology developers and policymakers toward a climate-resilient energy future.
